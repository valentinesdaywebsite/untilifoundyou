Valentine's Day Facts

❤️ In Finland, they celebrate Friends Day instead of Valentines Day. This day is spent with friends and not with lovers.
🌹 Red roses were the favorite flower of Venus, the Roman goddess of love.
💌 Origins in Ancient Rome: The holiday is believed to have originated from the Roman festival of Lupercalia.
🎁 Based on statistics, pet owners give gifts to their pets on this day.
🐶 Around 9 million pet owners buy Valentine’s gifts for their pets!
